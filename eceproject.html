Projet Ideas For Third and Fourth Year Student

      Robotics & Automation (Advanced)

  1. (Vision, AI integration, path planning)
  2. Autonomous Delivery Robot (Indoor)
  3. Map an indoor environment and deliver items between points.
  4. Skills: ROS (Robot Operating System), LiDAR/Ultrasonic, embedded control.
  5. AI-Based Surveillance Drone
  6. A drone with a camera to detect objects or track people.
  7. Skills: Raspberry Pi/Jetson Nano, OpenCV, wireless video transmission.
  8. Gesture Controlled Robotic Arm
  9. Use flex sensors/IMU to capture gestures and control a robotic arm wirelessly.
  10. Skills: Sensor fusion, wireless communication (Xbee/Bluetooth).
